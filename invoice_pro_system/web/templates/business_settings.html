How to connect another email provider

Use these details if you do not want to use Gmail Connect.

To send invoices from your own email address, enter your email provider's SMTP details. You will usually need the SMTP server, port, username, password or app password, and the correct TLS or SSL setting.

Gmail

`smtp.gmail.com`

Port `587` with TLS

Often needs an app password

Outlook / Microsoft 365

`smtp.office365.com`

Port `587` with TLS

Use your mailbox email as the username

Zoho Mail

`smtp.zoho.com`

Port `587` with TLS

May require an app-specific password

Custom domain email

Ask your hosting provider for SMTP details

Common providers include cPanel, Hostinger, Namecheap, and GoDaddy

If your normal password does not work, your provider probably requires an app password. This is common for Gmail, iCloud, Yahoo, and some Microsoft accounts.
